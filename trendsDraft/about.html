To HealMeal

ABOUT
HealMeal
HealMeal is a web application that prioritizes dietary health by offering personalized meal recommendations based on user preferences and health conditions. It helps users to pick meals that are appropriate based on what they like to eat and any health issues they might have.Â  It provides accessible tools for users to input their Body Mass Index (BMI), empowering informed decisions for improved physical fitness and overall well-being. With a user-friendly interface, HealMeal serves as a comprehensive resource, guiding users towards enhanced dietary habits and the achievement of wellness goals with convenience and accessibility.
